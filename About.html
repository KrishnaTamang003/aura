At Aura, we believe that beauty should be felt, not just seen. Each soap is a sculptural masterpiece, meticulously crafted to transform your daily ritual into an act of self-care.

Our Story

Born from a Vision of Mindful Beauty

Founded in 2023 in Kathmandu, Aura emerged from a simple question: What if everyday objects could be works of art? Our founder, a sculptor and aromatherapist, spent years perfecting the marriage of form and function.

Today, each Aura creation is hand-poured in small batches using sustainably sourced ingredients from the Himalayas. We work with master perfumers to develop scent profiles that transport, calm, and inspire.
